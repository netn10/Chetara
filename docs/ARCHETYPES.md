# Chetara (Chess Magic) — Draft Archetypes

The set's 10 two-color archetypes and the cards that neatly fit each one (color-legal + genuine mechanical synergy — build-around and payoff pieces, not generic filler). Colorless cards and lands can slot into any archetype.

**115 of 180 cards** are tagged to at least one archetype.
